About

Budget Buddy is the expense manager web application to automatically and securely track your monthly expenditure. It will find out your expends and classify them in categories such as in food, travel, shopping, etc. and how your expense patterns is changing over the time. Budget Buddy whole idea is to remove the manual effort and be responsible for managing the expenses on its own automatically. The best part of this is that being useful from day one and already having a backup of expense history. Accordingly user will get the statistics view of the expense and user can also generate the feedback report of his expenses. Our application will builds Statistics graphs based on your income and expenses over time or by particular category, specific budgets, and all accounts. Recommendations on the basis of expenditure For example If you are spending too much in any particular domain without any saving then it will recommend you to spend your money in mutual funds. Budget you finances to know where your money is going. Budget tracker is a complete online money management tools designed to keep track of all your transactions.

How It Works

A Budget Buddy for the modern age. Say no more to carrying paper envelopes. This virtual Budget Program keeps you on track with family and friends with the time tested envelope budgeting method. A proven budgeting system with powerful software tools you will love. Track envelope and account balances on the go and seamlessly sync your spending with multiple device and to the Budget Buddy website. The user will sign up for using the application and the user will login to enjoy the features of the application.
